What CGP Is

CGP is a framework for grassroots democratic governance that generates continuous, policy-level mandate through a triadic token system anchored to real-world fiat liabilities. Citizens deliberate on public policy in a distributed discussion substrate; politicians who deliver on ratified mandates are rewarded through an appreciating token whose value is bounded by the surplus generated by the very tax pool that underwrites the system.

Three token classes — Citizen, Agent, and Poli — move in reciprocal relation modelled on Ohm's Law, with no bilateral exchange permitted. The structure is designed to be capture-resistant by mathematical construction rather than by policed enforcement.

Why Now

A small number of AI companies and their principals are moving to concentrate unprecedented economic, scientific, and military decision-making power in the hands of a few dozen people. Existing representative democracy — a single binary vote every three or four years — cannot match the resolution or speed of decision-making that AI-empowered centralised actors are now capable of. The machinery of consent is too slow, too coarse, and too easily captured.

CGP is an attempt to build governance infrastructure sized for the challenge: continuous, policy-level, grassroots, cryptographically anchored, and structurally resistant to the capture modes that have disabled previous attempts.

Variants at a Glance

Four application variants are currently identified. All share the phylum-level invariants: triadic token structure, PoD and PoR as distinct consensus events, equal-share dividends for deliberation participants, emergent reciprocal supply against an anchor asset, transparency as a safety property, and free-speech discussion substrate.

Relationship to v0.3

The v0.3 single-document draft is superseded by this suite. Material changes from v0.3 include:

  • Proof of Ratification (PoR) named as a distinct consensus event from Proof of Deliberation (PoD).
  • Engineered inflation and deflation schedules removed; reciprocal supply dynamics are emergent.
  • Citizen dividend specified as equal-share Citizen Coin minting at PoD, independent of bid size.
  • Token ratio (Poli/Citizen) identified as a live, real-time accountability signal.
  • Application-variant structure introduced; four variants treated as peers within a common phylum.
  • Governance of the protocol separated from the protocol specification itself.
